## **Verification Requirements**

### **For Each Program, Verify:**
1. **Official Source**: Government website, DMV, law enforcement agency
2. **Implementation Year**: Exact year program started
3. **Coverage Type**: Statewide vs Local vs Pending
4. **Local Details**: Specific counties/cities for local programs
5. **Current Status**: Active, pending, or discontinued

### **Data Quality Standards**
- ✅ **Verified**: Has official government source
- ⚠️ **Needs Verification**: Source unclear or outdated
- ❌ **Unverified**: No official source found
